Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to bring the aquatic lessons of nature right to your home, offices, and youth groups! The Great Outdoors has so many different activities to offer, but what about teamwork? WhiteWater River Rafting is one of the best ways to give your closest ones an exhilarating chance to be the best team they can be!

Audience

  • Are you an anxious boss looking for a way to unify your office?
  • Are you a stressed out parent tired of all the stay-at-home-summer-vacation bickering from the kids?
  • Are you a worried youth group leader who just wants to show your kids what it means to be humble and strong?
Then look no further!

Typography

Heading Font: Work Sans

Work Sans is classy and professional looking, without overbearing the reader with formality as would a font with serifs. It is still fun with it's line weight not overly childish like a comic sans

Paragraph Font: Work Sans Light

For a professional website, fonts should match in style. The body copy is the same as the header so as not to distract from the content.

Normal paragraph example

The best Whitewater Rafting in Colorado, White Water Rafting Company offers rafting on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs. Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond.

Colored paragraph example

Trips vary from mild and great for families, to trips exclusively for physically fit and experienced rafters. No matter what type of river adventures you are seeking, White Water Rafting Company can make it happen for you.
